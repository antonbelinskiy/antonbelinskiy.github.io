    About

    RavenX is the most advanced charity crypto token, and the only blockchain company to provide donations to the Binance Charity with every transaction in more than 200 jurisdictions. RavenX gives digital asset holders the best for those in need and for themselves: instant donations to the Binance Charity while being owner of their RavenX Tokens which they can use for purchasing NFTs and for transactions among RX holders. 2% of every transaction is sent directly to the Binance Charity wallet, 2% is redistributed to holders and 2% is burned
